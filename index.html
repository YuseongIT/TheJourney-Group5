Check Out Our Featured Resorts!

The Park-Silang, Cavite





Accommodations at The Park are designed to provide the utmost comfort and relaxation, with spacious rooms featuring luxurious amenities such as bath tubs and private jacuzzis or plunge pools. Guests can also bring their furry companions along, as The Park is a pet-friendly hotel and resort.

Domescape, Batangas





As the first of its kind in the country, Domescape combines the comfort of a luxury hotel with the raw beauty of nature, providing guests with a truly immersive experience. esigned to blend seamlessly with the natural surroundings, each dome offers all the comforts of home, including plush bedding, modern amenities, and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Tyvo Resort, Antipolo





Tyvo Resort is renowned for its exquisite event venues, perfect for hosting a wide range of celebrations, from birthdays and debuts to weddings and corporate events. With a capacity to accommodate up to 200 guests, the resort features breathtaking views of Metro Manila's skyscrapers and the shimmering waters of Laguna de Bay, creating an unforgettable backdrop for any occasion.

Anilao Awari Bay Resort, Batangas





Nestled in the picturesque coastal town of Batangas, Philippines, Anilao Awari Bay Resort offers a luxurious and tranquil retreat for discerning travelers. With its stunning architecture and breathtaking views, this 5-star resort is the perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Tanay Hideaway, Rizal





Situated amidst the majestic Sierra Madre Mountain Range, Tanay Hideaway boasts panoramic views that soothe the soul and inspire the spirit. Guests can revel in the natural beauty of their surroundings, with lush greenery stretching as far as the eye can see and a cool breeze that whispers through the trees.

Caliraya Lake House, Laguna





Surrounded by nature's bounty, The Lake House provides guests with an opportunity to reconnect with the great outdoors and indulge in eco-friendly living without sacrificing modern comforts. With its picturesque location overlooking Caliraya Lake, guests can unwind and rejuvenate amidst breathtaking views and fresh mountain air.

MORE ABOUT OUR FEATURED RESORTS

"The Park-Silang is a hotel and events venue in Southern Luzon. Located in between the beautiful cities of Tagaytay and Laguna which is only an hour away from Metro Manila, the property is situated in a secluded and calming environment which is a perfect place to unplug and recharge from the hustle and bustle of the city life."

"DOMESCAPE is the first of its kind in the Philippines. A family vacation domes that we decided to share whenever not in use. We want our guests to experience raw nature w/o having to sacrifice comfort. Our geodesic dome is more than just a tent, we want our guests to experience the comfort of staying in a luxury hotel with the great outdoors as its lobby. Most parts of the property were left untouched to provide a truly authentic experience. Domescape is for adventure seekers and nature lovers."

"Tyvo Resort is the perfect private place is an event venue and private resort fit for your leisure and special occasions. Tyvo Resort offers swimming parties and events like birthdays, debut, weddings, and all other events. It can accommodate guests of up to 200 an features a breathtaking view of Metro Manila Skyscrapers and the Laguna de Bay!"

"Tanay Hideaway has got the goods and by the goods, we mean sprawling lawns, trees, and open air. The spacious villas are kept away from each other making sure you get your privacy. You’ve got a great view of the Sierra Madre Mountain Range and a cool breeze that will greet you throughout your stay. But if you aren’t too keen on a vacation that’s just sitting around and reading a book, there are a lot of other activities you can try too."

"From what started as a quaint wooden hut by the lakeshore, this weekend home grew into a beautiful guesthouse in the middle of the lush green forest of the Caliraya Lake Watershed. Over the years, it has been a venue for friends and relatives to reconnect, share special family recipes, reminisce and create fond memories. Most of all, it gave guests a chance to reconnect with nature and escape from the stresses of city life. The Lake House is now open to those who wish to find a quiet place to experience eco-friendly living without sacrificing the practical pleasures of home life."

"Escape the city hustle and bustle and indulge in quality time with loved ones at Anilao Awari Bay, nestled in Mabini Batangas, just a 3-hour drive south from the metro. Perched on a private beach along the serene Balayan Bay and adjacent to the renowned diving spot MatuPoint, our resort offers a plethora of activities including swimming, diving, island hopping, and more, perfect for family and friends seeking respite from urban life. With a tranquil ambiance and stunning 180o views of the bay, guests can unwind with sunset vistas and spa massages after days filled with beachside fun. Boasting professional diving crews, top-notch equipment, and cozy accommodations featuring modern amenities and traditional Ifugao-inspired décor, Anilao Awari Bay promises an unforgettable retreat from the city grind."

Rules and Regulations

    User Conduct:

    • Users must adhere to respectful and courteous behavior towards fellow users, tour guides, and website administrators.
    • Any form of harassment, discrimination, or hate speech will not be tolerated.
    • Users are responsible for the accuracy and truthfulness of the information they provide on the website.

    Tour Guide Conduct:

    • Tour guides must possess proper licenses, permits, or certifications required for conducting tours in their respective locations.
    • Tour guides are expected to provide accurate and reliable information about the destinations they cover.
    • Any promotional or commercial activities must be transparently disclosed and approved by the website administration.

    Booking and Payment:

    • Users must provide accurate information when booking tours.
    • Payment must be made through secure and approved payment gateways provided by the website.
    • Cancellation policies should be clearly outlined for both users and tour guides.

    Content Guidelines:

    • User-generated content, such as reviews and comments, must be relevant, constructive, and respectful.
    • Content that violates intellectual property rights or contains illegal or inappropriate material will be removed.
    • Tour descriptions, images, and other content provided by tour guides must accurately represent the actual experience.

    Safety and Liability:

    • Tour guides are responsible for ensuring the safety of participants during tours.
    • Users are responsible for following safety guidelines and instructions provided by tour guides.
    • The website administration is not liable for any accidents, injuries, or damages that may occur during tours.

    Privacy and Data Protection:

    • The website must comply with all applicable data protection laws and regulations.
    • Users' personal information must be handled securely and used only for the purposes outlined in the website's privacy policy.

    Feedback and Complaints:

    • Users should provide honest feedback and report any issues or concerns they encounter during tours.
    • Complaints will be investigated promptly, and appropriate actions will be taken to address them.

    Legal Compliance:

    • The website must comply with all relevant laws and regulations governing the tourism industry, including licensing, taxation, and consumer protection laws.

    Modification of Terms:

    • The website administration reserves the right to modify these rules and regulations at any time. Users will be notified of any changes, and continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the modified terms.

    Termination of Services:

    • The website administration reserves the right to terminate services for users or tour guides who violate these rules and regulations or engage in misconduct.

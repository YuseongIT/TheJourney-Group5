Caliraya’s Self-sustaining & Most Famous Lake House

The Lake House is a sprawling private vacation home, nestled on the banks of a mountain lake in the area known as the Caliraya Watershed and Nature Reserve. It’s a beautiful Balinese-inspired home, but what’s even more impressive about this lake house is how little it impacts on the forest and lake around it. It’s 100 percent solar-powered and water is sourced from the ground.
